System uptime

2020-05-04
reference
132 words 1 min read

High Availability is the property of a system which measures its uptime. When you measure your system’s uptime and are confident that no matter what, at minimum your system remains up x hours per year you can offer it as a commitment to the users of your system by signing a SLA (Service-level agreement)

System uptime is usually expressed as three nines, four nines or five nines. You can translate it into uptime or downtime hours per year by multiplying it with the total number of hours in a year (8760).

Uptime levelUptime hours per yearDowntime hours per year
99.9%$$(8760 * \frac{99.9}{100}) = 8751.24$$(8760 - 8751.24) = 8.76
99.99%$$(8760 * \frac{99.99}{100}) = 8759.12$$(8760 - 8759.12)=0.88
99.999%$$(8759.91 * \frac{99.999}{100}) = 8759.91$$(8760 - 8759.91) = 0.09
